Our Trips

From calm morning paddles to Class IV rapids, every JJRC trip is crafted around your comfort level and your hunger for adventure. All trips include certified guides, safety gear, and one unforgettable story.

Beginner

Ogun River Float

A calm, 2-hour guided paddle along the scenic Ogun River — perfect for first-timers, families, and anyone who wants to enjoy the river without the rush. You'll learn basic paddle techniques, read the water, and finish with a riverside snack.

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Difficulty: Class I – II
  • Group size: 4 – 12 people
  • Price: ₦15,000 / person
Intermediate

Gurara Rush

A half-day run through the Gurara River's mid-section, featuring Class III rapids, natural rock formations, and stretches of flat water for recovery. This is where most returning guests level up. Prior paddling experience is recommended but not required — your guide handles the hard calls.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Difficulty: Class III
  • Group size: 4 – 8 people
  • Price: ₦28,000 / person
Expert

Benue Gorge Challenge

A full-day assault on one of Nigeria's most demanding river sections. The Benue Gorge route includes back-to-back Class IV drops, a technical 300m canyon run, and a mandatory pre-trip safety briefing. Not for the faint of heart — but the views from the bottom of the gorge are something else entirely.

  • Duration: 7 hours
  • Difficulty: Class IV
  • Group size: 4 – 6 people
  • Price: ₦55,000 / person
