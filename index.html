Nigeria

My country Nigeria, the Federal Republic of Nigeria, is a massive country in West Africa, sitting between the Sahel up north and the Gulf of Guinea down south. People call it the "Giant of Africa," and trust me, that's not just a flex. It's the most populated country on the entire continent, we're talking over 242 million people, and a huge chunk of that is young people. All of that spread across 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory. And it's not just big in population, Nigeria genuinely carries weight: culturally, economically and politically. It's one of the countries that the rest of the region actually listens to, which is why it plays such a big role in organizations like ECOWAS.
